Technical Support Guidelines

Common Issues and Solutions:

1. Product not turning on:
   - Check power connection
   - Try different power outlet
   - Contact support if issue persists

2. Software installation problems:
   - Ensure system meets minimum requirements
   - Disable antivirus temporarily during installation
   - Download latest version from our website

Escalation Path:
- Level 1: Basic troubleshooting (all agents)
- Level 2: Advanced technical support (specialized team)
- Level 3: Engineering team (for critical issues)

Response Time SLAs:
- Critical issues: 2 hour response
- High priority: 4 hour response
- Normal issues: 24 hour response

Shipping and Delivery FAQ

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 day delivery.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes, all orders receive a tracking number via email once shipped.

Q: What if my package is lost or damaged?
A: Contact support immediately with your order number. We will:
   - Investigate with the carrier
   - Reship the product if confirmed lost
   - Offer refund or replacement for damaged items

International Shipping:
- Additional customs fees may apply
- Delivery times vary by country
- Some product restrictions may apply
